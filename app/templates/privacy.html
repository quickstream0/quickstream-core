Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 13, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Your privacy is our top priority. QuickStream is designed to respect your data and provide a secure streaming experience.

The app may use temporary identifiers (such as device model, operating system version, or crash logs) only to improve performance and fix bugs. These identifiers are anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

We DO NOT collect: Name, Email address, Phone number, Location data, Payment information, or any personally identifiable information.

2. Third-Party Services

The app relies on third-party content providers to deliver movies, TV shows, live sports, and TV channels. These providers may collect information under their own privacy policies. We have no control over their data collection practices.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through QuickStream. The app itself does not share any information with these providers.

3. How We Use Information

The limited anonymous data we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing app functionality and features
  • Improving app stability and fixing bugs
  • Enhancing overall performance
  • Understanding usage patterns to improve user experience

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal data with third parties. We do not have any personal data to share in the first place. Your privacy is completely protected.

5. Data Security

The app does not store sensitive data on our servers. All data remains locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the app from vulnerabilities and ensure a safe streaming environment.

Data Storage: QuickStream uses local storage on your device only. We do not maintain any external databases containing user information.

6. Children's Privacy

QuickStream does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains content suitable for all ages, but parental guidance is recommended for unsupervised viewing. Since we collect no personal information, there is no risk of child data exposure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Continued use of the app after any changes means you accept the updated policy.

We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal information, you have the following assurances:

  • No data to access, correct, or delete
  • No data portability needed
  • No risk of data breach affecting your personal information
  • Complete anonymity while using the app
