Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about QuickStream

What is QuickStream?

+

QuickStream is a free online streaming app available on Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux. Watch Movies, TV Shows, Live Sports, and Live TV channels. Enjoy our massive library of 100,000+ films and access to 1,000+ premium channels - completely free!

Is QuickStream really free on all platforms?

+

Yes! QuickStream is free on all platforms - Android, iOS, Windows, Mac, and Linux. No subscriptions, no hidden fees. We support the app through non-intrusive advertisements.

Is QuickStream safe to use?

+

Yes, QuickStream is safe to use. We prioritize user privacy and security. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

Can I sync my progress across devices?

+

Yes! Create a free account to sync your watch history, favorites, and progress across all your devices - Android phone to Windows PC to iPad, seamlessly.

What are the system requirements?

+
  • Android: 7.0 or higher
  • iOS: iOS 13.0 or later
  • Windows: Windows 10 or 11
  • macOS: macOS 10.15 or later
  • Linux: Ubuntu 20.04+, Fedora 34+, or similar

Do I need to create an account?

+

No account is required to start streaming! However, creating a free account allows you to save watch history, create favorites, and sync across multiple devices.
