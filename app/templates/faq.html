What is QuickStream?

QuickStream is an online streamming app for watching Movies, TV Shows, Sports, and live TV.
With a huge library of 100,000+ films and 1000+ Premium sports and entertaiment channels.

Is QuickStream safe to use?

Yes, QuickStream is safe to use. We do not violate your privacy or collect your data that we haven't inform you about.

Why can't I install QuickStream?

Please go to your device settings and enable "allow install from unknown sources" then try again.

Is QuickStream available for iOS?

No, QuickStream is currently available for Android devices only. Hopefully we will consider other platforms on future updates.

Can I install QuickStream on Android TV?

Yes you can install QuickStream on Android TV but note that the app is not fully compatible with Android TV. Our team is working on a separate Android TV version that may be released in the near future.

Can I use external player with QuickStream?

Yes you can integrate external video player like VLC Player or MX Player and others.
